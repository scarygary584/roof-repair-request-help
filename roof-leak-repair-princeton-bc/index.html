Leak Details to Mention

Clear details help the provider understand the request.

  • Active dripping or recurring water stains
  • Ceiling stains, wall stains, or damp spots
  • Attic moisture, damp insulation, or musty odor
  • Leak after rain, snow, snow melt, or wind
  • Leak around chimney, vent, skylight, pipe boot, flashing, or roof valley
  • Whether buckets or containers are being used indoors

Avoid unsafe access

Do not climb onto a wet, icy, steep, damaged, or unsafe roof. If possible, take photos from the ground or a safe indoor location.

Roof Leak FAQ

What should I do if water is coming through the ceiling?

If safe, move valuables, use containers for drips, and keep away from electrical hazards. Contact emergency services first if immediate danger exists.

What should I have ready when calling?

Share the leak location, when it started, weather conditions, roof type if known, and safe photos if available.

Is emergency roof leak repair guaranteed?

No. Provider availability varies, and urgent or emergency availability is not guaranteed.
