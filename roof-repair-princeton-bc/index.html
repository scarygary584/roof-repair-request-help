Roof Concerns You Can Mention

Give clear details so the provider can decide whether the request is a fit.

  • Roof leaks or ceiling water stains
  • Missing, lifted, cracked, or curling shingles
  • Metal roof concerns, loose panels, or fastener questions
  • Flashing concerns around chimneys, walls, skylights, and valleys
  • Roof vent or pipe boot leaks
  • Storm, wind, branch, or impact damage
  • Roof age, wear, and repeated leak concerns

Safety first

Do not climb onto a wet, icy, steep, damaged, or unsafe roof. If there is immediate danger, structural concern, fire, injury, electrical danger, or flooding near electrical fixtures, contact emergency services first.
