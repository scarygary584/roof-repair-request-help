Urgent Roof Concerns

These are common reasons people submit urgent roof requests.

  • Sudden roof leak or water entering the home
  • Storm damage after heavy rain, wind, or debris impact
  • Missing shingles after wind
  • Damaged flashing near walls, chimneys, skylights, vents, or valleys
  • Fallen branch or impact damage
  • Questions about temporary protection or roof tarp help

Contact emergency services first when needed

If there is electrical danger, structural collapse risk, fire, injury, active flooding near electrical fixtures, or immediate danger, contact emergency services first.

Emergency Roof Request FAQ

Is emergency roof repair guaranteed?

No. Emergency availability is not guaranteed, and provider availability varies.

What should I do while waiting to hear about availability?

If safe, move valuables away from active drips and use containers indoors. Avoid unsafe roof access.

When should emergency services be contacted first?

Contact emergency services first for immediate danger, fire, injury, electrical danger, structural collapse risk, or active flooding near electrical fixtures.
