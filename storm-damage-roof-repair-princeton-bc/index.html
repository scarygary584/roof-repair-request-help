Storm Damage Details to Share

Mention what you can safely observe.

  • Wind damage, lifted materials, or missing shingles
  • Heavy rain leaks or water stains appearing after a storm
  • Branches, debris, or visible impact areas
  • Hail-like impact concerns if relevant
  • Damaged flashing, vents, pipe boots, skylights, or roof edges
  • Whether water is entering the home now

Photos from safe locations

Take photos from ground level or a safe indoor location if possible. Do not climb onto a wet or damaged roof.
